Privacy policy

Your data is our data, your data security is our first priority.

We do not store any kind of data permanently. All the data you provide us is deleted after 30 days of the work completion.

All the conversation we have from the requirement gathering to website launch is kept private and secure. We make sure that your data is kept private during the journey we work together.

Any kind of payments you make during the development phase, we never ask you to provide any card information, neither we store it at our end.

We do not share any kind of information about our customers and their business with anyone.

We do not discuss about your project ideas with anyone, we are ready for any kind of NDA (Nondisclosure Agreement) if needed.

We will seek for your approval to showcase your work in our portfolio. We will never add anything in our showcase unless approved by you.
