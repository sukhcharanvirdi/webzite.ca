How we work

We have divided our work into three steps.

Step 1

The first step is when you reach out to us for the website development. In this step, we get some background infromation about your work and your website development requirements. This is the most crucial step for us and our clients. In this step. we try to gather as much as information we can, so that our clients get what they want.

At the same time, we discuss all the prerequisits with you and the cost estimation is also provided along with the payment schedule. We further dicuss with you regarding setting up your business email addressess and provide the cheap and best solutions.

If your website does not have high complexity and dynamic operations, we can help you setting up your website at almost free yearly cost.

Step 2

This is the development step where we start working on your webite and will keep sharing the progress with you as per the schedule. We will be working on different things in this step, which include:

  • Setting up website domain
  • Setting up hosting
  • Logo design
  • Website design
  • Website development
  • Website testing
  • Setting up business emails

Step 3

This is the most awaited step. After setting up everything as per the business requirements, we start launching the website. This is the most exciting step towards reaching your goals. After we are done, you can discuss if you want us to maintain your website.

We also provide maintanance work, which includes modifying the website pages from time to time.
